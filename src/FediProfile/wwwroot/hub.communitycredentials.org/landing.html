Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I have to pay for anything?

Nope. Hub is completely free for people who earn badges. The organizations that issue you badges are the ones using the paid platform.

Do I need to be technical to use Hub?

Not at all. If you can check your email, you can use Hub. There's no app to install, no settings to configure — just sign in and your profile is ready.

How do my badges get into my profile?

When an organization awards you a badge through Community Credentials, it appears in your Hub wallet automatically. You don't need to do anything extra.

Can people actually verify my badges are real?

Yes! Every badge on your profile links back to the issuing organization and includes cryptographic proof. Anyone — an employer, a recruiter, anyone — can click and confirm it's genuine.

What if I volunteer with multiple organizations?

Perfect setup for you. Badges from all your Community Credentials organizations collect on your single Hub profile. One link, every badge, all verified.
